Mappings are Digital Vector's reading of the VCE Applied Computing: Software Development study design (2025–2028); KK codes follow the conventional ordering of each outcome, not official VCAA numbering. Verify against the current study design before publishing.
Cohort averages and Section A answers are drawn from the VCAA examiner's reports for 2020–2025. Section A "avg" is the proportion answering correctly. Each paper = 100 marks (A 20 · B 20 · C 60). 2020, 2021, 2022 and 2023 sat under the previous (2020–2023) study design; their questions are mapped to the nearest current key knowledge, so treat those years as indicative rather than exact. Note the 2020 paper ran under the COVID adjusted study design and used a different section split (B = 4 questions, C = 14 questions). Where VCAA accepted multiple Section A options (2022 Q2, Q14), the proportion correct is the combined figure.
